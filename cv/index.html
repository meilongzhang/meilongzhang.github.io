Research Areas

  • My research focuses on the cross-section of machine learning and the human brain. I analyze concept rule learning through machine learning application on electrophysiological signals in the human brain.
  • I have also worked on natural language processing in the development of sentiment analysis models, and integrated supervised and unsupervised systems to create robustness for computer vision tasks.
  • I am also interested in understanding intelligence, and have therefore also worked on several personal projects in deep reinforcement learning.
